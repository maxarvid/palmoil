Palm company throws temper tantrum in Liberia

BY Max Anderson

Published December 00, 2018

What does it take for a powerful company to show its true colors? For Golden Veroleum Liberia Inc., it was a letter and time. Golden Veroleum is a palm oil company, with large concessions on the Liberian coast since 2010. This summer it ended its membership in the Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil, an industry-led multi-stakeholder group which aims to ensure the sustainability of palm oil, because of a complaint filed against the company in 2012.

Liberian Palm Oil Concessions

There are eight palm oil concessions in Liberia. Golden Veroleum's concessions are located in Sinoe and Grand Kru Counties.
